# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-12

This Privacy Policy explains how the Styings Adhan Bot handles information when you use the bot.

## Information We May Collect

The bot may process information needed to provide its features, such as:
- Discord user identifiers needed to recognize command usage
- Settings or preferences you choose while using the bot
- Location or timezone information if you use location-based features

## How We Use Information

We use this information to:
- provide prayer time and related bot features
- personalize bot behavior based on your preferences
- improve reliability and troubleshoot issues

## Data Storage

Any data collected is used only for the operation of the bot. We do not sell your personal information.

## Third Parties

The bot may use third-party services for basic functionality, such as Discord and external APIs for prayer time data. These services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

## Your Choices

You may stop using the bot at any time. If you want your data removed, contact the bot maintainer through the project’s support channel or GitHub repository.

## Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact the project maintainer through the repository or support channel.
